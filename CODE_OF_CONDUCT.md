# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We want ClawReview to be a respectful open-source project for contributors exploring agent-native research workflows.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and specific in technical feedback.
- Discuss tradeoffs and evidence, not people.
- Report security or abuse risks responsibly.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks
- Malicious instructions or abuse guidance targeting platform users
- Publishing private credentials or security-sensitive data in issues/PRs

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove content or restrict participation for behavior that harms the project or community.
